概念
DESIGN CONCEPT

TOOOOT origins from exploring the concept of “clone”.

Cloning, it is just executing a command for a computer. On the other hand, cloning without a computer focuses on the story behind each work.

It requires ceaseless experiments, experience accumulation, and emotion. The meaning behind each works is irreplaceable by a computer.

We look for the concept of “clone” in our daily life. We found that during food production, decorating nozzles, cookie mold, and spaghetti all use a

single mold to achieve the cloning effect, but because of human interaction and minor factors, it creates surprising results. Decorating Nozzle is a

metaphor of duplicating which we choose to describe the core concept that TOOOOT searches for ——unique values of individuals.

產品與包裝設計
PRODUCT & PACKAGE DESIGN

Decorating Nozzles is a metaphor of duplicate. The extended and winding shape of objects being squeezed out means the process of production.

The shapes of completed works vary from each other. That’s what the core concept that TOOOOT searches for ——

unique values of individuals. We had tried various ingredients and process, using improved nozzle to produce tablewares.

The final products is a set of tableware with red copper spoon and pottery handle.

TOO - Packages for single tableware and for gift box use round corners to promote the amiability of visual and touch.

The paper is chosen in ivory to increase the appetite, and the sharp polygonal lines and folded lines are decreased by being made into one

piece forming.The sponge inside is to protect the tableware of china.

Each of single package is printed three kinds of cut shapes of decorating nozzles in brand, those with a red stamp mean

the cut shape used in the tableware inside.The gift box packaging is designed by the main core of brand:

decorating nozzles, the characteristics about curve, equal rough and round corner, and presented by convex.

視覺識別系統
VISUAL IDENTITY DESIGN

Visual identity system of TOOOOT is designed by the features of decorating nozzles.We apply its special texture, and rounded lines of the same thickness to our logo and gift packages; The section of decorating nozzle is supporting graphic which is presents in dessert shop.
